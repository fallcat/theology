# Open Questions

Top-level questions not yet assigned to a specific tradition or module. These represent research directions for future work.

## Theological Method

- **How should historical-critical and theological readings of Scripture relate to each other?** The research system assumes both textual observation and theological interpretation. But the boundary between "what the text says" and "what it means" is itself contested (cf. Gadamer, *Truth and Method*; Brevard Childs, *Biblical Theology of the Old and New Testaments*). A methodology question that affects every question file.

## Cross-Tradition

- **Is "salvation" a universal religious category, or does applying it across traditions distort non-Christian frameworks?** Christianity, Buddhism, Islam, and Hinduism all have concepts sometimes translated as "salvation," but the underlying anthropologies and cosmologies differ so sharply that the shared label may obscure more than it reveals. This question should be addressed before the comparative layer grows further.
